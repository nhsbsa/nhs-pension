Things to be aware of

You may not realise that there are employment benefits offered to you that can increase or reduce the amount of pension you could get in retirement.

Benefits that will increase your pension

  • working beyond your "normal pension age"
  • increasing your salary

Benefits that could reduce your pension

  • retiring before your "normal pension age"
  • taking a lease car
  • getting a bike through the cycle to work scheme
  • working part time
  • childcare voucher scheme

It is a good idea to sign in regularly to keep an eye on how your pension is growing.

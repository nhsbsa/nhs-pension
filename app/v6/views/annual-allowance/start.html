NHS Pensions

Check your annual allowance pension savings

Your annual allowance is the most you can save in all your pension pots in a tax year (6 April to 5 April) before you have to pay tax.

Use this service to:

  • Check how much you have saved each year in your NHS pension
  • Help you work out if you have gone above the allowance
  • Share your information with a tax advisor

You'll need:

  • your NHS pensions member number sometimes called SD number
  • to sign in using NHS Login or GOV.UK Verify

What to do if you haven’t used NHS Login or GOV.UK Verify

This takes about 10 minutes to get set up. You’ll then be able to use it to access other NHS and government services.

Your pension information is updated throughout the tax year. It is your employers responsibility to keep your information up to date.

The current tax year is 2019/2020.

  1. 6 April

    Start of the tax year.

  2. 6 July

    Your employer must send us your pay and pension membership information from the last year (2018/2019).

  3. 31 July

    You must tell us if you want to use the NHS pension scheme to pay for a tax charge from the tax year before the last tax year (2017/2018).

    Find out more about scheme pays on our website.

  4. 6 October

    We will send pension savings statements to certain members to confirm the value of savings over the last tax year (2018/2019).

  5. 31 January

    Employees must send thier self-assessment tax return for the last tax year (2018/2019) to HM Revenue & Customs (HMRC).

  6. 5 April

    End of the tax year.

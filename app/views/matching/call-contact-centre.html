Why is there a problem?

There are many reasons why there could be a problem with your NHS pension record such as:

  • Your personal details have changed
  • You have moved house
  • You have got married
  • Your employer has entered incorrect details

What happens next?

If you are currently working in the NHS you may be able to update this information in your Electronic Staff Record (ESR). If you don't have access to ESR you need to contact your pensions officer or employer to get your information updated.

Alternatively you can call the NHS pensions helpline.

  • Member helpline: 0300 330 1346
  • Member helpline: 0191 279 0571
  • Calling from overseas: 0044 191 279 0571

It can take up to 10 days to update your details.

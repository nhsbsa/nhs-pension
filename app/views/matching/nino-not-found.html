We cannot find your National Insurance (NI) number in our pension records

Why is there a problem?

There could be a number of reasons why we cannot find your NI number, such as:

  • us holding incorrect information
  • your employer hasn't updated your details
Get help to find your NI number

Your National Insurance number is on your National Insurance card, a benefit letter, a payslip or a P60. For example, ‘QQ 12 34 56 C’.

What happens next?

Your NI number needs to be updated by your employer, you will need to either speak to your:

  • pensions officer
  • employer

It can take up to 10 days to update your details.

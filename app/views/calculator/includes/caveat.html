This estimate is not guaranteed and is based on current law, and pay information provided by your employer.

Our website has guidance on how your estimate is calculated.

If you are currently paying pension contributions we have assumed a pay increase of 2% each year.

We have used an assumed inflation figure of 0.5% each year.

What's included in my estimate?

The calculation does not take account of the following:

  • Any Early Retirement Reduction Buy Out (ERRBO) you may have purchased
  • Any Additional Pension you may have purchased
  • Any ‘Pensions on Divorce’ Debit or Credit that applies to you
  • Any Scheme Pays debits that you may have applied for
  • Any benefits you may have transferred in from a Club CARE scheme
